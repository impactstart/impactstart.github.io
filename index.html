About Us

Impact from the Start is in the early stages of developing a nonprofit dedicated to supporting early childhood development through access to healthcare, maternal mental health resources, and caregiver education. We believe that by ensuring children receive proper medical care and their caregivers are supported, we can foster healthier futures for entire families.

Engagement & Feedback

We’re looking for passionate individuals, families, and professionals to join our movement. Whether you’re a parent, educator, healthcare provider, or advocate, we want to hear from you. Share your ideas, get involved, and help us shape a future where every child has an equal opportunity to thrive.

We are currently developing ways for you to get in touch. Stay tuned for updates on how you can contact us to learn more or contribute to our growing initiative.

Our Story

We’re a small, dedicated team working hard to bring this vision to life. As we grow, we appreciate your support, ideas, and patience. Your involvement will help us make a meaningful impact.

Impact from the Start was created because I believe that early childhood development starts with proper healthcare and strong caregiver relationships. As someone passionate about children's well-being, I want to make sure families have the resources they need—starting before birth. This mission is deeply personal, and I’m committed to making change happen.
